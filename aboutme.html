~ About Me ~
Hi, I'm Fasteroid! Nice to meet you!

I'm a student pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. My current studies are taking place at University of Delaware, but I'll be transferring to Wilmington University next semester for much needed quality of life improvements.

I began writing my first original lines of code early to mid 2014, in a little-known language called Expression 2 from the popular sandbox game Garry's Mod. Learning E2 was a very trial-by-error sort of process, since there was very little information online about it. In the very beginning, I literally learned by changing single characters of public scripts and observing the effects if it ran. By 2015 however, once I really started understanding basic syntax, it turned into a massive power trip and I was instantly hooked. I've been bending digital reality to my will ever since.

As a result of this learning style, I've gotten pretty good at learning from few examples and reverse-engineering code. Perhaps you'll find this of interest, future employers… :)

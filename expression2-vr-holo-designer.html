VR Hologram Structure Designer
When it comes to creating custom 3D models in E2, holograms are really the only way to do it. Since their addition to Expression 2 over 10 years ago however, they've still been relatively difficult to work with for developers.

Picture something cool, like a car, cat, or boat with a tophat. Next, break that cool thing into primitive shapes, like spheres, domes, cubes, cylinders, pyramids, etc. Finally, enter the coordinates, angles, and scales of those shapes into your E2.

Surely nothing can go wrong with the last step, right?

Not even Notch could do this reliably for Minecraft's mobs! The most iconic mob of the game, the creeper, originated from a failed pig model, where the height and length of the torso were mistakenly swapped.

I envisioned a better way. What if instead of typing in the coordinates, you could just intuitively position and scale things yourself, with no numbers required? What if better yet, there was a library that could also automate spawning these structures for you?

This is where I now introduce it to you: A VR hologram "CAD" program for Garry's Mod, that works in conjunction with a library system that can spawn these creations with a single line of code.

Now isn't that fantastic? Not to mention convenient! Now get out there and make your dreams with this thing!
